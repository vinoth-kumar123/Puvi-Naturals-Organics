Why it's worth seeking out

Grown the slow way, on purpose

Every variety here starts in a farmer's hands, not a factory.

Native seed, preserved

Every variety here is a true heirloom, not a hybrid — seeds passed down through farming families, not patented in a lab.

No chemical inputs

No synthetic pesticide, no chemical fertiliser — grown the way paddy was grown before either existed.

Stone milled, not polished

Husked gently to keep the bran and nutrition intact, instead of polishing it away for a whiter grain.

Bought from small farmers

Sourced directly from native-seed farmers across Tamil Nadu, at a fair price, with no middlemen in between.
